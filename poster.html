Abstract

CrimeLens is an AI-powered surveillance platform that analyzes live and recorded camera feeds using Computer Vision and deep-learning models to automatically detect suspicious and criminal activity in real time. The system scores each event by priority, raises instant alerts, and presents them to security teams through connected web and mobile dashboards — replacing continuous manual monitoring with a scalable, AI-driven workflow while keeping a human in control of every critical decision.

Introduction

Surveillance cameras have become a core part of modern security infrastructure across cities, facilities, and public spaces. Yet the volume of video they generate far exceeds what human operators can watch effectively — studies show that attention and detection accuracy drop sharply after only a short period of continuous monitoring, so important incidents are often missed or noticed too late. The rapid progress of Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision now makes it possible to analyze video streams automatically and recognize abnormal or criminal behaviour as it happens, creating a strong need for intelligent systems that support security teams instead of relying on manual watching alone.

Problem Statement

Traditional surveillance systems mainly depend on human operators to monitor camera feeds continuously. This is time-consuming, error-prone, and may lead to delayed response when suspicious or criminal activities happen. In crowded or sensitive areas, it becomes difficult to detect incidents quickly and accurately using manual monitoring only.

Our Professional Solution

CrimeLens uses Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision to analyze video streams and detect suspicious or criminal behaviours automatically. It processes camera input, identifies potential threats, and generates real-time, priority-scored alerts — turning hours of manual monitoring into instant, actionable insight for security teams.
